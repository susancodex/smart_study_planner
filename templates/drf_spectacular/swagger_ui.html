How to authenticate

  1. 1 Use the demo credentials on the right — or register a new account via POST /api/auth/register/.
  2. 2 Call POST /api/auth/login/ with username and password to get your access token.
  3. 3 Click the Authorize 🔒 button below, enter Bearer <token> and click Authorize.
  4. 4 All endpoints are now unlocked — try them out!
